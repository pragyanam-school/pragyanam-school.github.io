Philosophy

At Pragyanam School, co-scholastic activities are not just a tick box exercise; they are an integral part of our life skills development program. We believe that learning through sports and other activities is an invaluable experience that cannot be matched.

Engaging in sports teaches students essential skills such as teamwork, resilience, coping with stress, and building interpersonal relationships. Our philosophy extends beyond mere participation; each activity is thoughtfully designed to align with our lesson plans, nurturing life skills in a structured and purposeful manner. We have integrated co-scholastic activities into our assessment matrix, recognizing their importance in the holistic growth of our students.
