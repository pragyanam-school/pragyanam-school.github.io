Introducing Pragyanam Enrichment Program (PEP)

About PEP

At Pragyanam School, we understand the importance of providing students with opportunities beyond the regular school day to further their holistic development. That's why we offer an exceptional extended program called the Pragyanam Enrichment Program (PEP). Designed to nurture and develop life skills, PEP is a well-crafted and robust program that goes beyond simply managing children or helping them with their homework.

Program Details

PEP runs from Monday through Friday, from 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM, providing an enriching and engaging experience for our students outside of regular school hours. During this time, students have the opportunity to delve into a focused and comprehensively designed curriculum that nurtures their life skills and allows them to explore their passions.

Nurturing Life Skills

PEP focuses on the holistic development of students by fostering essential life skills. Through carefully curated activities, projects, and experiences, we aim to equip our students with the necessary skills and qualities to navigate the challenges of the modern world. Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, communication, problem-solving, collaboration, and other vital life skills.

Discovering Passions

PEP offers students a wonderful opportunity to explore their interests and discover their passions. By engaging in a variety of activities, students can broaden their horizons, develop new skills, and uncover hidden talents. Whether it's through art, music, dance, STEM, or other engaging areas, PEP provides a platform for students to pursue their interests beyond the confines of the regular school day.

A Supportive Environment

At Pragyanam School, we strive to create a nurturing and supportive environment for our students. PEP is facilitated by experienced and passionate educators who understand the importance of individualized attention and guidance. We foster an inclusive and encouraging atmosphere that allows each student to thrive and grow.

Parent Involvement

We believe that parental involvement plays a vital role in a child's educational journey. Through regular communication and collaboration, we keep parents informed about their child's progress and participation in the Pragyanam Enrichment Program. We encourage parents to actively engage with their child's interests and experiences in PEP, fostering a strong partnership between home and school.

Discover the Power of Pragyanam Enrichment Program

PEP at Pragyanam School is more than just an after-school program. It is a transformative experience that enables students to develop holistically, discover their passions, and acquire essential life skills. Join us in this enriching journey and witness the remarkable growth and potential of your child.
