Join us on this remarkable journey of exploration, discovery, and lifelong learning.

Admission Process

  • Step 1: Complete the online registration/application form by visiting Registration Link.
  • Step 2: Upon submission, you will receive an SMS or call notification to schedule a school tour on the designated date and time.
  • Step 3: Student Interaction:
    • Pre-Nursery/Nursery: Direct admissions with no interaction required.
    • LKG/UKG: Interaction-based admission process.
    • Grade I & IV: Interaction with the Head of the Primary Wing.
    • Grade V onwards: Assessment-based admission process with an interaction component.
  • Step 4: Admission Decision: Once the assessment and interaction process is complete, the admission decision will be made. If accepted, the submission of admission fees and caution money within 7 days of the decision is required to secure the admission.

Please note that the number of seats in each class is limited, and registration alone does not guarantee admission.

Age Criteria for Admissions

  • Pre-Nursery: 2 years as of 31st March for the applied academic session.
  • Nursery: 3 years as of 31st March for the applied academic session.
  • LKG: 4 years as of 31st March for the applied academic session.
  • UKG: 5 years as of 31st March for the applied academic session.
  • Grade 1: 6 years as of 31st March for the applied academic session.

Questions and Answers

Q: What documents are needed for admission?

A: The required documents include Aadhar card of parent, birth certificate/Aadhar of child, soft copy of photos (child and parent), residence and office address of parent, any allergy information for the child, blood group of the child, and contact details of both parents.

Q: What is the application deadline?

A: Admissions are processed on a rolling basis until seats are filled.

Q: What is the class size and student-teacher ratio?

A: The class size is designed as per CBSE standards, with a student-teacher ratio of 1:10 for pre-primary, 1:15 for Grade 1-5, and 1:30 for Grade 6 onwards.

Q: What extracurricular activities are offered?

A: Pragyanam School offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including basketball, tennis, badminton, swimming, athletics, taekwondo, table tennis, skating, and co-curricular activities such as music, art & craft, reading, drama, yoga, and dance.

Q: Does the school provide meals to the students?

A: Yes, nutritious meals are provided at Pragyanam School. We prioritize serving healthy food and strictly avoid junk food, maida, and bread.

Q: Which board is the school affiliated with?

A: Pragyanam School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

Q: From which grade will my child have the option to learn a foreign language? Which foreign languages do you teach?

A: The option to learn a foreign language begins from Grade 1. Pragyanam School offers Spanish and French, in addition to English and Hindi.

Q: Does the school have CCTV cameras and GPS-enabled buses?

A: Yes, Pragyanam School has CCTV cameras installed throughout the campus and buses equipped with GPS for enhanced safety and security.

We hope these answers provide the information you were seeking. If you have any further questions or require clarification, please do not hesitate to contact our admissions team.
