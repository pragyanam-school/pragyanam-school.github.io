Infrastructure - Pragyanam School

Section 1: A Modern and Ample Facility

Spanning over 1 lac sq ft, Pragyanam boasts a modern and ample facility that provides a conducive environment for learning. Our well-designed classrooms, laboratories, and specialized spaces are thoughtfully arranged to optimize the educational experience of our students. With spacious and well-lit areas, we create an atmosphere that fosters creativity, collaboration, and effective learning.

Section 2: Spacious Playgrounds for Sports Enthusiasts

At Pragyanam, we understand the importance of physical fitness and sports in the holistic development of our students. Our 2.5-acre playground is a haven for sports enthusiasts, hosting facilities for tennis, basketball, cricket, and football. This expansive space allows our students to engage in various sports activities, promoting teamwork, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle.

Section 3: Indoor Sports Area for Year-Round Activities

To ensure sports activities are not hindered by weather conditions, Pragyanam features an indoor sports area. Equipped with badminton courts, basketball facilities, and table tennis, this dedicated space allows students to continue their sports training and practice throughout the year.

Section 4: Swimming Pool and Infirmary for Health and Safety

The well-being of our students is of utmost importance to us. Pragyanam provides a swimming pool for students to develop their aquatic skills and engage in water-based activities under the supervision of trained instructors. Additionally, we have an infirmary equipped to handle any minor medical emergencies and provide immediate care when needed.

Section 5: A Separate Building for Pre-Primary Students

Recognizing the unique needs of our pre-primary students, Pragyanam has a separate building exclusively designed for their early learning journey. These vibrant classrooms, equipped with child-friendly amenities, create a nurturing and engaging environment that supports their growth and development.

Section 6: Well-Equipped Labs for Practical Learning

At Pragyanam, we emphasize hands-on learning experiences. Our well-equipped labs, including the science lab, computer lab, and art studio, provide students with the necessary resources to explore, experiment, and unleash their creativity. These specialized spaces are designed to enhance practical learning and develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Section 7: Dedicated Dance, Arts, and Drama Rooms

We believe in the importance of fostering artistic expression and creativity in our students. Pragyanam features dedicated dance, arts, and drama rooms where students can explore their talents, develop their skills, and engage in various artistic endeavors. These spaces provide the perfect platform for self-expression and allow students to showcase their creativity.

Section 8: Indoor Auditorium for Performances and Events

Our state-of-the-art indoor auditorium is designed to host performances, events, and gatherings. With a capacity to accommodate up to 400 people, this spacious facility serves as a platform for our students to showcase their talents, engage in cultural activities, and build their confidence through public speaking and presentations.

Section 9: Play Area with Swings for Pre-Primary and Primary Children

We understand the importance of age-appropriate play areas for our young learners. Pragyanam features specially designed play areas with swings and equipment tailored to the needs of our pre-primary and primary children. These spaces provide a safe and enjoyable environment for our students to engage in physical activity and develop their motor skills.

Section 10: Enhanced Security and Facilities

Pragyanam ensures the safety and security of our students. Our premises are guarded 24x7 by trained security personnel, providing a secure environment for learning. Additionally, we provide live CCTV access to parents, allowing them to stay connected with their child's educational journey. Moreover, our school is equipped with 100% power backup to ensure uninterrupted learning experiences.
