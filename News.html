Best Emerging School in India Award by BW Education

We are thrilled to announce that Pragyanam has been recognized as the Best Emerging School in India by BW Education. This prestigious award reflects our unwavering commitment to providing exceptional education and creating a nurturing environment where children can thrive. We are proud to be acknowledged for our efforts and will continue to strive for excellence in all aspects of our educational journey.

Recent News & Events

STEM Fest - 1st, July 2023

Pragyanam hosted an exhilarating STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) festival, known as StemFest. This event provided our students with an immersive and hands-on experience in various STEM disciplines.

Pragyanam is now an official test partner of the University of Waterloo

Pragyanam is proud to partner with the esteemed University of Waterloo. This partnership offers our students unique opportunities to participate in various international-level math and computing competitions.

Parenting Workshop on 1 July 2023

As part of our commitment to holistic education, Pragyanam organized an insightful parenting workshop. This interactive session provided parents with valuable guidance and strategies to support their child's overall development, well-being, and academic journey.
