Leadership Team

Raj Singhal - Profile

Raj Singhal, an esteemed alumnus of IIT Delhi, serves as a vital bridge between curriculum and engaging activities that nurture essential life skills. He collaborates closely with teachers to develop innovative lesson plans that transform learning units into enjoyable experiences. Raj's vision is to create an environment where every learning opportunity promotes the growth of life skills. By incorporating international practices, such as seeking feedback from students on teacher performance, he ensures a dynamic and student-centric approach.

Inspired by his own children, Raj understands the transformative power of fun and engaging education. His dedication to their growth and development sparked his passion for creating an educational institution that cultivates a love for learning. Raj's personal experiences as a parent continue to motivate him to foster a nurturing and stimulating environment at our school.

Purvesh Sharma - Profile

Purvesh Sharma, a certified life coach and passionate education advocate, plays a pivotal role in enhancing parent engagement and teacher training. He meticulously plans parent engagement processes, fostering strong partnerships between families and the school community. Additionally, Purvesh leads insightful parenting workshops, empowering parents with valuable tools and strategies. His expertise extends to providing comprehensive teacher training in behavioral management techniques.

Driven by the desire to bridge the gap between the education system and real-life skills, Purvesh draws inspiration from his own journey as a parent. Witnessing the challenges his son faces in the traditional education system motivated him to create an institution that prioritizes practical knowledge and holistic development. Purvesh's personal commitment to empowering parents, teachers, and students shapes the foundation of our school.

Ashish Aggarwal - Profile

Ashish Aggarwal, an accomplished alumnus of IIT Delhi and IIM Calcutta, brings his expertise in safety and engagement to our school. He diligently oversees the security and well-being of every child, implementing robust safety protocols. Additionally, Ashish liaises with educational authorities, including CBSE and the Haryana Education Department, to ensure compliance and excellence in our institution.

Motivated by his own child's educational journey, Ashish recognized the need for a transformative approach to education in India. He founded our school with a vision to create an environment that nurtures wise and resilient individuals capable of conquering life's challenges. Ashish's commitment to safety, engagement, and educational excellence shapes the core values of our institution, ensuring a holistic and empowering learning experience for all students.

Amita Bhardwaj - Head of School

Amita Bhardwaj, a seasoned educator with over 30 years of experience in the field of education, brings a wealth of expertise to her role as the Head of School. Her career has been marked by successful tenures across respected educational brands, serving diverse age groups. For the past decade, Amita has led the Curriculum and Delivery team at Footprints, India's most beloved pre-school chain. She is also one of the few certified trainers in India for the Highscope Curriculum and a dedicated volunteer course leader with Landmark Education, the world's largest training company.

With a deep commitment to staying updated on best practices in education, Amita has completed several courses at Harvard University, further enhancing her knowledge and expertise. She is known for her extensive experience working with children of all age groups. Amita's dedication to education extends beyond her professional life, as she is the proud mother of a son and a daughter who have successfully completed their undergraduate studies and are now employed in reputable companies.

Yatharth Verma - Profile

Yatharth Verma, a graduate from the prestigious Delhi College of Engineering, chose to follow his passion for education and make a meaningful contribution to the field. During his Teach for India Fellowship, Yatharth made a significant impact at the grassroots level by leading various projects both within and outside the school. One of his noteworthy achievements was being a founding member of the third free English Medium Secondary School in Pune, established through a successful public-private partnership.

After his fellowship, Yatharth focused on teaching international curriculums such as IB (International Baccalaureate) and IGCSE (International General Certificate of Secondary Education) for Science. Alongside his teaching responsibilities, he took on diverse roles within the school. His most recent position was as a Maths and Physics educator for the Middle and Senior School at Shiv Nadar School in Gurgaon.

Yatharth is also deeply involved in nurturing his own initiative for STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education. He leads a team of teachers and interns, organizing and conducting numerous programs that have impacted the lives of thousands of individuals. In addition, Yatharth is a wellness enthusiast, holding a level-2 certification as a Yoga teacher with the Government of India's AAYUSH ministry. He is also a voluntary faculty member with the Art of Living Foundation.

At Pragyanam, Yatharth plays a hands-on role in implementing the vision of converting each learning unit into engaging activities that nurture life skills in children. He works closely with teachers, leveraging his diverse experiences and passion for education to create a dynamic and transformative learning environment.
