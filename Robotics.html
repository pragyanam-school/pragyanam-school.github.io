Robotics Lab @ Pragyanam

Introducing the Robotics Lab at Pragyanam School

At Pragyanam School, we believe in providing our students with practical, hands-on knowledge and igniting their passion for Computer Science, Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (CS-STEM). Our Robotics Lab offers an excellent platform for students to explore the multi-disciplinary field of robotics, encompassing mathematics, physics, electronics, mechanics, and computer programming.

Key Features of our Robotics Lab:

  • Promoting Practical Learning: The Robotics Lab focuses on imparting practical, activity-based, and hands-on knowledge to our students. Through engaging projects and challenges, students have the opportunity to design and construct their own robotic creations, fostering teamwork, creativity, concentration, and problem-solving skills.
  • Multi-Disciplinary Approach: Robotics integrates various subjects, including mathematics, physics, electronics, mechanics, and computer programming. By actively participating in robotics projects, students gain a deeper understanding of these subjects and their real-world applications.
  • Learning by Doing: Our Robotics Lab follows the "Learning by Doing" methodology, encouraging students to explore and experiment. Through hands-on activities, they develop problem-solving, decision-making, and scientific inquiry skills, as well as the ability to comprehend abstract concepts in a playful and interactive manner.
  • Collaboration with LEGO: We are proud to partner with LEGO, a renowned name in the field of educational robotics. LEGO Education provides innovative learning solutions that enhance students' engagement and understanding of CS-STEM concepts. Our Robotics Lab utilizes LEGO robotics kits and resources to facilitate immersive learning experiences for our students.

Frequency and Participation:

Robotics activities are conducted once a week as part of our optional activities. Students have the opportunity to choose robotics as their preferred co-scholastic activity, allowing them to delve deeper into the fascinating world of robotics and its applications.
