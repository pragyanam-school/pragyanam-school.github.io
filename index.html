What we do

At Pragyanam, we are on a mission to nurture happiness and satisfaction in every child. Through our unique approach, we focus on developing essential WHO life skills that empower students to thrive in any situation and be happy and satisfied. By seamlessly integrating these skills into our engaging curriculum, we ensure that learning becomes a joyful journey of self-discovery and growth.

Meet Our Visionary Founders

Raj Singhal

Raj Singhal, an IIT Delhi alumnus and parent to 19-year-old twins, believes in fun and engaging education. As the founder of Footprints, India's beloved preschool chain, he has nurtured over 25,000 children. Raj aims to bring the same enriching experience to Pragyanam, fostering a love for learning in students.

Purvesh Sharma

Purvesh Sharma is an IIT Delhi alumnus, passionate about education. With 25 years of volunteering experience at Landmark Education, he's trained over 5000 people as a certified life coach. As a dedicated parent with a keen belief in practical life skills, he aims to bridge the gap between education and real-life challenges.

Ashish Aggarwal

Ashish Aggarwal, an IIT Delhi and IIM Calcutta alumnus and a parent to an 11-year-old, envisions transforming the Indian education system. He believes schools should nurture mature and wise individuals who can fearlessly tackle life's challenges, advocating for a holistic approach to education for a successful and fulfilling future.
